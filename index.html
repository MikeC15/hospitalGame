You are the surgeon's blue gloved hand. You will see a mix of blood cells and bleeders in the patient. You have 30 seconds to save your patient. Hover over the brightest red bleeders to stop your patient from dying, gain some health back and they will turn blue. Keep an eye on the patients health level, it will drop every second. If they finish surgery with health 0 or under they will die, and it will be on your permanent record. If they have health left at the end of surgery, they survive and you will gain money to spend on upgrades. Health CAN go negative as long as you resuscitate them in time! Careful, if you kill 5 patients you will lose your license!
